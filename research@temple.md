## My Research at Temple University

### 1. Genome Characterization and Population Genetics in *Plasmodium* spp.
<p align="justify">
I am currently characterizing the nuclear genome of <i>Plasmodium gonderi</i> to perform comparative genomics, phylogenomics, and timing analyses within the genus <i>Plasmodium</i>, from an evolutionary perspective of the genus. On the other hand, I am looking for evolutionary signatures in <i>P. vivax</i> and <i>P. falciparum</i> populations, with the perspective of expanding the epidemiological analysis of malaria in humans.
